3.6.7.1.d We already found

\begin{align*} f'(x)&=3\dfrac{2-x}{2\sqrt{3-x}}.\\ \end{align*}

This is undefined at \(x=3\text{.}\) Indeed,

\begin{align*} \ds\lim_{x \rightarrow 3^-} 3\dfrac{2-x}{2\sqrt{3-x}}&=-\infty, \end{align*}

so \(f(x)\) has a vertical tangent line at \((3,0)\text{.}\)
