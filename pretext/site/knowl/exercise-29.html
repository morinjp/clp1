Exercise 1

Suppose \(\displaystyle\lim_{x \rightarrow a} f(x)=0\) and \(\displaystyle\lim_{x \rightarrow a} g(x)=0\text{.}\) Which of the following limits can you compute, given this information?

  1. \(\displaystyle\lim_{x \rightarrow a} \frac{f(x)}{2}\)
  2. \(\displaystyle\lim_{x \rightarrow a} \frac{2}{f(x)}\)
  3. \(\displaystyle\lim_{x \rightarrow a} \frac{f(x)}{g(x)}\)
  4. \(\displaystyle\lim_{x \rightarrow a} f(x)g(x)\)
