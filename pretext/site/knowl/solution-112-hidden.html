No. IVT says nothing about functions that are not guaranteed to be continuous at the outset. It's quite easy to construct a function that is as described, but not continuous. For example, the function pictured below, whose equation format is somewhat less enlightening than its graph: \(f(x)=\left\{\begin{array}{ll} -\frac{26}{5}x+65,&10 \leq x \lt 15\\ -\frac{26}{5}x+91,&15 \leq x \leq 20 \end{array}\right.\text{.}\)
