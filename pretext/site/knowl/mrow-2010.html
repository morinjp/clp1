\begin{align*} -28x+2y+2xy'+2yy'&=0\\ \end{align*}

Plugging in \(x=1\text{,}\) \(y=3\text{:}\)

\begin{align*} -28+6+2y'+6y'&=0\\ y'&=\frac{11}{4} \quad\mbox{\textcolor{red}{ at the point $(1,3)$}}\\ \end{align*}

Differentiating the equation \(-28x+2y+2xy'+2yy'=0\):

\begin{align*} -28+2y'+2y'+2xy''+2y'y'+2yy''&=0\\ 4y'+2(y')^2+2xy''+2yy''&=28\\ \end{align*}

At the point \((1,3)\text{,}\) \(y'=\dfrac{11}{4}\text{.}\) Plugging in:

\begin{align*} 4\left(\frac{11}{4}\right)+2\left(\frac{11}{4}\right)^2+2(1)y''+2(3)y''&=28\\ y''&=\frac{15}{64} \end{align*}
