\begin{gather*} \diff{}{x} \{ \sin x \} \Big|_{x=0} =\lim_{h\rightarrow 0}\frac{\sin h-\sin 0}{h} =\lim_{h\rightarrow 0}\frac{\sin h}{h} \end{gather*}
