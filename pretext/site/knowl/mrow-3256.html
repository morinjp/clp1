\begin{align*} T_{2n}(x)&=\sum_{k=0}^n \frac{(-1)^k}{(2k)! }\cdot x^{2k}\\ \end{align*}

If \(n=100\) and \(x=\dfrac{5\pi}{4}\text{,}\) this equation becomes

\begin{align*} T_{200}\left(\frac{5\pi}{4}\right)&=\sum_{k=0}^{100} \frac{(-1)^k}{(2k)! }\cdot \left(\frac{5\pi}{4}\right)^{2k} \end{align*}
