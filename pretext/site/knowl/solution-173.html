The rate of change in this case will be the relationship between the heat added and the temperature change. \(\displaystyle\lim_{h \rightarrow 0} \dfrac{T(j+h)-T(j)}{h} \approx \dfrac{T(j+1)-T(j)}{1}=T(j+1)-T(j)\text{,}\) or the change in temperature after the application of one joule.
