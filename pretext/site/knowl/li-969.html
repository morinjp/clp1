Item 3.3.4.8.b
Since \(T(t)=30-25e^{kt}\text{,}\) the temperature of the tea is \(20^\circ\) when
\begin{align*} 30-25 e^{kt}&=20\\ e^{kt}&=\frac{10}{25}\\ kt&=\log\left(\frac{10}{25}\right)\\ t&=\frac{1}{k}\log\frac{2}{5}\\ &=\frac{5\log(2/5)}{\log(4/5)}\\ &\approx{20.53\;\mathrm{ min}} \end{align*}
