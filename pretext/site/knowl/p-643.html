Suppose \(f(x)=2x\) and \(g(x)=\frac{1}{x}\text{.}\) Evaluate the following limits.

  1. \(\displaystyle\lim_{x \rightarrow 0} f(x)\)
  2. \(\displaystyle\lim_{x \rightarrow 0} g(x)\)
  3. \(\displaystyle\lim_{x \rightarrow 0} f(x)g(x)\)
  4. \(\displaystyle\lim_{x \rightarrow 0} \dfrac{f(x)}{g(x)}\)
  5. \(\displaystyle\lim_{x \rightarrow 2} [f(x)+g(x)]\)
  6. \(\displaystyle\lim_{x \rightarrow 0} \dfrac{f(x)+1}{g(x+1)}\)
