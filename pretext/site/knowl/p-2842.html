2.14.2.22.a In order to make \(f(x)\) a little more tractable, let's change the format. Since \(|x|=\left\{\begin{array}{rl} x&x \geq 0\\ -x&x \lt 0 \end{array}\right.\text{,}\) then:

\begin{equation*} f(x)=\left\{\begin{array}{rl} -x^2&x \lt 0\\ x^2&x\ge 0.\end{array} \right. \end{equation*}
