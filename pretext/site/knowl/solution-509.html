Solution 3.4.11.10.1

From the text, the quadratic approximation of \(e^x\) about \(x=0\) is

\begin{equation*} e^x \approx 1+x+\frac{1}{2}x^2 \end{equation*}

So,

\begin{equation*} e=e^1 \approx 1+1+\frac{1}{2}=2.5 \end{equation*}

We estimate \(e \approx 2.5\text{.}\)

Remark: actually, \(e \approx 2.718\text{.}\)
