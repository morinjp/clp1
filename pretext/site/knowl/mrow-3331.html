\begin{align*} |f(x)-T_n(x)| &= \left|\frac{f^{(n+1)}(c)}{(n+1)!}(x-a)^{n+1}\right|\\ \end{align*}

for some \(c\) strictly between \(x\) and \(a\text{.}\) In our case, \(n=5\text{,}\) \(a=11\text{,}\) \(x=11.5\text{,}\) and \(f^{(6)}(c)=\dfrac{6!(2c-5)}{c+3}\text{.}\)

\begin{align*} \left|f(11.5)-T_5(11.5)\right|& = \left|\dfrac{1}{6!}\left(\dfrac{6!(2c-5)}{c+3}\right)(11.5-11)^{6}\right|\\ & = \left|\dfrac{2c-5}{c+3}\right|\cdot\frac{1}{2^6} \end{align*}
