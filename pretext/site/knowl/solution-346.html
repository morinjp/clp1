Solution 2.12.2.9.1

We use the chain rule, remembering that \(a\) is a constant.

\begin{align*} \diff{}{x}\left\{\frac{1}{a}\arctan\left(\dfrac{x}{a}\right)\right\}&= \frac{1}{a}\cdot\frac{1}{1+\left(\frac{x}{a}\right)^2}\cdot \frac{1}{a}\\ &=\frac{1}{a^2+x^2} \end{align*}

The domain of arctangent is all real numbers, so the domain of \(f(x)\) is also all real numbers.
