While you're working with sums, it's easy to mistake a constant for a function. The sum given in this question is some number: \(\pi\) is a constant, and \(k\) is an index-- if you wrote out all 100 terms of this sum, there would be no letter \(k\text{.}\) So, the sum given is indeed a number, but we don't want to have to add 100 terms to get a good idea of what number it is.
