To differentiate, we use the chain rule. Remember \(\ds\diff{}{x}\left\{\arcsec x\right\} = \dfrac{1}{|x|\sqrt{x^2-1}}\text{.}\)

\begin{align*} \diff{}{x}\left\{\arcsec(-x^2-2)\right\}&= \dfrac{1}{|-x^2-2|\sqrt{(-x^2-2)^2-1}}\cdot(-2x)\\ &=\dfrac{-2x}{(x^2+2)\sqrt{x^4+4x+3}}. \end{align*}
