If you're having a hard time seeing why the sum is 26, instead of 25, think of it this way: there are thirty numbers in the collection \(\{1,2,3,4,5,6,\ldots,29,30\}\text{.}\) If we remove the first four, we get \(30-4=26\) numbers in the collection \(\{5,6,\ldots,30\}\text{.}\)
