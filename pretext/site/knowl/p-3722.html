The quadratic approximation of a function \(f(x)\) about \(x=a\) is

\begin{align*} f(x) &\approx f(a)+f'(a)(x-a)+\frac{1}{2}f''(a)(x-a)^2\\ \end{align*}

We compute derivatives.

\begin{align*} f(0)&=e^0=1\\ f'(x)&=2e^{2x}\\ f'(0)&=2e^0=2\\ f''(x)&=4e^{2x}\\ f''(0)&=4e^0=4\\ \end{align*}

Substituting:

\begin{align*} f(x) &\approx 1+2(x-0)+\frac{4}{2}(x-0)^2\\ f(x) &\approx 1+2x+2x^2 \end{align*}
