Let us turn this around a little bit. Consider the premise of a 90s action film  3  — a bus must travel at a velocity of no less than \(80km/h\text{.}\) Being a bus, it is unable to go faster than, say, \(120km/h\text{.}\) The film runs for about 2 hours, and let's assume that there is about thirty minutes of non-action — so the bus' velocity is constrained between \(80\) and \(120km/h\) for a total of \(1.5\) hours.
