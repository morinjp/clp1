\begin{align*} \diff{}{x}\{\sin x\}&=\cos x\\ \diff{}{x}\{\sin(13x)\}&=13\cos(13x)\\ \diff{}{x}\left\{\frac{1}{13}\sin(13x)\right\}&=\frac{13}{13}\cos(13x)=\cos(13x)\\ \diff{}{x}\left\{\frac{7}{13}\sin(13x)\right\}&=7\cos(13x)\\ \end{align*}

So, an antiderivative of \(7\cos(13x)\) is \(\dfrac{7}{13}\sin(13x)\text{.}\)
