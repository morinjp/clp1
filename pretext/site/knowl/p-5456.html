Newton's method 1 , also known as the Newton-Raphson method, is another technique for generating numerical approximate solutions to equations of the form \(f(x)=0\text{.}\) For example, one can easily get a good approximation to \(\sqrt{2}\) by applying Newton's method to the equation \(x^2-2=0\text{.}\) This will be done in Example C.1.2, below.
