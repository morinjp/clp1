For any real number \(x\text{,}\) we will show that \(f(x)=f(x+4\pi)\text{.}\)

\begin{align*} f(x+4\pi)&=\sin(x+4\pi)+\cos\left(\frac{x+4\pi}{2}\right)\\ &=\sin(x+4\pi)+\cos\left(\frac{x}{2}+2\pi\right)\\ &=\sin(x)+\cos\left(\frac{x}{2}\right)\\ &=f(x) \end{align*}

So, \(f(x)\) is periodic.
