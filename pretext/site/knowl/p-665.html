Let \(f(x) = \frac{1}{x}\) and \(g(x) = \frac{-1}{x}\text{.}\)

  1. Evaluate \(\displaystyle\lim_{x \rightarrow 0} f(x)\) and \(\displaystyle\lim_{x \rightarrow 0} g(x)\text{.}\)
  2. Evaluate \(\displaystyle\lim_{x \rightarrow 0} [f(x)+g(x)]\)
  3. Is it always true that \(\displaystyle\lim_{x \rightarrow a} [f(x)+g(x)]= \displaystyle\lim_{x \rightarrow a} f(x)+\displaystyle\lim_{x \rightarrow a} g(x)\text{?}\)
