The fourth-degree Maclaurin polynomial for \(f(x)\) is

\begin{align*} T_4(x)&=f(0)+f'(0)x+\frac{1}{2}f''(0)x^2+\frac{1}{3!}f'''(0)x^3+\frac{1}{4!}f^{(4)}(0)x^4\\ \end{align*}

while the third-degree Maclaurin polynomial for \(f(x)\) is

\begin{align*} T_3(x)&=f(0)+f'(0)x+\frac{1}{2}f''(0)x^2+\frac{1}{3!}f'''(0)x^3 \end{align*}
