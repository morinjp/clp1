Solution. By way of preparation, we'll start with a simpler problem. Suppose that you will have to make a single $420 payment \(n\) months in the future. The simpler problem is to determine how much money you have to deposit now in an account that pays an interest rate of \(100 r\%\) per month, compounded monthly 4 , in order to be able to make the $420 payment in \(n\) months.
