We want \(v(t)\) to be the speed of the peregrine's dive, so we should convert that to metres per second:

\begin{equation*} 325\;\frac{\mathrm{km}}{\mathrm{hr}}\cdot \left(\frac{1000\;\mathrm{m}}{1\;\mathrm{km}}\right)\left(\frac{1\;\mathrm{hr}}{3600\;\mathrm{sec}}\right)=\frac{1625}{18}\;\frac{\mathrm{m}}{\mathrm{s}} \end{equation*}
