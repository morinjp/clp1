Let \(f(x)=x^{1/3}\) and \(x_0=27\text{.}\) Then

\begin{align*} f(x)&=x^{1/3} & f'(x)&=\frac{1}{3}x^{-2/3} & f''(x)&=-\frac{2}{9}x^{-5/3}\\ f(27)&=27^{1/3}=3 & f'(27)&=\frac{1}{3}\cdot\frac{1}{3^2}=\frac{1}{27} & f''(27)&=-\frac{2}{9}\cdot\frac{1}{3^5}\\ \amp\amp\amp\amp\amp=-\frac{2}{2187} \end{align*}

so that, with \(x=25\text{,}\)

\begin{align*} 5^{2/3}&=f(25) \approx f(27)+f'(27)(25-27) =3-\frac{2}{27}\\ \amp\approx 2.9259 \mbox{ (linear approx)}\\ 5^{2/3}&=f(25)\approx f(27)+f'(27)(25-27)+\frac{1}{2} f''(27)(25-27)^2\\ &=3-\frac{2}{27}-\frac{1}{2}\cdot\frac{2\cdot 4}{2187} \approx 2.9241 \mbox{ (quadratic app)} \end{align*}
