\begin{align*} 0&=\diff{}{w}\left\{w^2+\dfrac{2000}{w}\right\}\\ &=2w-\dfrac{2000}{w^2}\\ 2w&=\dfrac{2000}{w^2}\\ w^3&=1000\hskip-3pt\\ w&=10\\ \end{align*}

\begin{align*} \ell&=3w=30\\ h&=\frac{1500}{w^2}=15. \end{align*}

The dimensions of the box with minimum surface area are \(10 \times 30 \times 15\text{.}\)
