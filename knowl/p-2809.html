  1. Compute \(y'\) at this point.
  2. It can be shown that \(y''\) is negative when \(x=1\text{.}\) Use this fact and your answer to 2.14.2.18.a to make a sketch showing the relationship of the curve to its tangent line at \((1,2)\text{.}\)
