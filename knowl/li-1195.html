Item
When \(0 \lt c \lt 1\text{,}\) we know that \(1 \gt e^{-c} \gt e^{-1}\) and \(8 \leq 8+c^2 \lt 9\text{,}\) so
\begin{align*} \left|\frac{e^{-c}}{6(8+c^2)}\right| &=\frac{e^{-c}}{6(8+c^2)}\\ & \lt \frac{1}{6 |8+c^2|}\\ & \lt \frac{1}{6\times 8} = \frac{1}{48} \lt \frac{1}{40} \end{align*}
as required.
