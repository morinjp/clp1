Solution 2.9.4.18.1

Using the Product rule,

\begin{align*} y'&=\diff{}{x}\{e^{4x}\}\tan x+e^{4x}\sec^2 x\\ \end{align*}

and the chain rule:

\begin{align*} &=e^{\textcolor{red}{4x}}\cdot\diff{}{x}\{\textcolor{red}{4x}\}\cdot\tan x+e^{4x}\sec^2 x\\ &=4e^{4x}\tan x+e^{4x}\sec^2 x \end{align*}
