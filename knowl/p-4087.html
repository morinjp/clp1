We set \(f(x)=(8+x)^{2/3}\text{,}\) and choose \(a=0\) as our centre. Then \(f'(x)=\dfrac{2}{3}(8+x)^{-1/3}\text{,}\) so that

\begin{align*} (8.06)^{2/3}=f(0.06)&\approx f(0)+f'(0)\cdot 0.06\\ &=8^{2/3}+\frac{2}{3}8^{-1/3}\cdot 0.06\\ &=\sqrt[3]{8}^2+\frac{2}{3\sqrt[3]{8}}\cdot 0.06\\ &=2^2+\frac{2}{3\cdot 2}\cdot 0.06\\ &=4+\frac{1}{3}\cdot 0.06\\ &=4.02=\frac{402}{100} \end{align*}
